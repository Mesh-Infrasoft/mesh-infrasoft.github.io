Business Intelligence






Business intelligence is a collection of procedures, architectures, and technology that transform unstructured data into actionable information. It's a set of tools and services for turning data into useful intelligence and knowledge.

Final thoughts of BI:

These days, having a good business intelligence system is a must for any company. For today's fast-paced enterprises, keeping an attentive eye on the company's internal operations as well as its external health is important. The use of data to better the company should be part of the company's core values. Data is the oil of the twenty-first century, and BI is the lamp that extracts value from it.
