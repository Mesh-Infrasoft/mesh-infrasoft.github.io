Data Governance







In a nutshell, Data Governance is the process of establishing accountability and authority in all aspects of data. "Accountability for data items"
Problems that led to Data Governance:
1. Various versions of truth.
2. No ownership.
3. No data context/meaning.
4. Lack of structured documention.
Hence, to sort out all these problems at a single shot Data Governance has been rised.
